TOKEN OVERVIEW

Bitnou’s token (BNOU) is a cryptocurrency that serves as a utility token to support the BITNOU ecosystem.

BNOUs are not shares or securities of any kind, and they do not grant holders any ownership or other interest in Bitnou Group companies, affiliates, or subsidiaries.

Built on the Binance Smart Chain, Bitnou token (BNOU) is the stamina of the Bitnou ecosystem, created to fuel and self-sustain the ecosystem. Besides being able to trade the BNOU token, users can use it on the Bitnou CEX, as well as the BitnouSwap for DeFi, staking and farming profits.

BNOU serves several functions for the protocol, including staking in BNOU pools to earn rewards, rewarding users for adding liquidity to the BitnouSwap platform, as well as for Bitnou NFTs and all other future projects.
